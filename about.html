About EarthQuiz

EarthQuiz is a quick, fun way to test how well you really know our planet. One quiz, ten questions, covering everything from geography and climate to wildlife and natural wonders — no categories to pick, no long setup, just jump in and see how you score.

Whether you're a trivia lover, brushing up on general knowledge, or just want a quick mental challenge, EarthQuiz gives you a fast, satisfying way to learn something new about the world in just a few minutes.

How it Works

  • 10 Questions — A quick mix covering different aspects of our planet
  • One Question at a Time — Answer and move forward with the Next button — no going back, so choose carefully
  • Your Score — See your final result out of 10 as soon as you finish all 10 questions
  • Play Again Anytime — Click Play Again to restart the quiz and try for a better score

Why EarthQuiz?

Learning about the world shouldn't take effort to start. EarthQuiz keeps it simple — one short quiz, real facts, and a quick way to challenge yourself whenever you have a few free minutes.
